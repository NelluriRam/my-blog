Hitting back to the past, he is always a career enthusiastic dreaming about a well-driven career, putting all his efforts he archived much in his schooling and he always wanted to develop a product for which the world has its re-owned use, His enthusiasm towards building a product grew more stronger in his under graduation, so he started building a product using his minutely growing coding skills. Where there is positivity there is a negativity he always compare his knowledge with others and he underwent stress by the exceptional skills shown by his friends he started hating him by putting the work back and feeling bad for his performance. One fine day ,He looked himself in the mirror and started talking to his inner self that why he always thought about others, He started making up his brain towards positivity telling himself that everyone will have one strength and you are not exceptional Finally, He decided not to compare himself with others but he always screwed up watching his friend's immense talent .From that day He started working even more harder But the results don't satisfied him He asked his friend "why he is not scoring much marks".
